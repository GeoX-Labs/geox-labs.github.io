About

The Geometry of Deep Learning is the study of the geometry of the function of a deep network. Rather than focusing on the geometry of the input domain, it focuses on how the deep network geometrically manipulates the input domain. The aim of GeoX Labs (GEOmetric eXplainability Labs) is to take this geometric perspective to explain the properties and behaviours of deep networks. With the intention to develop frameworks to facilitate the development and deployment of deep networks.

The main thrust of our explorations will utilise theory motivated in the context of continuous piecewise affine deep networks, namely the Spline Theory of Deep Learning. In this setting, deep networks naturally partition their input space into linear regions. The properties and arrangements of these regions effectively captures many of the properties of deep networks.

Our goal with GeoX Labs is to develop a community exploring the geometry of deep learning, provides demos which highlight how deep networks can be intuitively grasped through this geometric lens, and translate theoretical explorations into practical tools to control and study deep networks in deployment.
